In the swiftly varying globalized economy and society, Knowledge is the only motivating force. Competence, quality and quantity of expertise human resources determine their proficiency in the international market. Sprouting of knowledge as driving factor results in both opportunities and challenges for the countries with good levels of management education. Globalization has paved a multi-dimensional impact on the education system of Country. It promotes new paraphernalia and techniques in the area of Management education. Therefore for leading Management Institutes, talent is perhaps the only truly sustainable competitive advantage. In light of this, Prestige Institute of Management Dewas, one of the leading Management school in central India is been staffed by the best possible executive talents. Institute offer highly focused learning, based on practical research and groom students into 'people susceptible' managers, robust to perform outstandingly in the corporate world. Drawing on the experience, vision of a powerful model for business school success and wisdom gained by Shri N.N Jain Chairman Prestige Education Society, the sprawling lush green campus was founded in Dewas in the year 2001 . Our location being in the heart of the industrial belt at Dewas one of the biggest industrial zones in Madhya Pradesh, helps students cultivate a business oriented attitude. It is anticipated that in the future it would advance into a world class renowned business school known for its vow to high powered quality education and research. We at PIMD are dedicated towards developing exceptionally outstanding entrepreneurs and professionals who epitomize humanistic ideals and are socially accountable stewards who will strengthen the society. Our primary objective is to broaden the management education and horizons, and to develop specialist skills within our students for their career enhancement .My aim at PIMD is to ensure that our students come out as winners rationally, ethically, intellectually, and professionally. I believe in motivating students consistently for their career progression and skills development. Towards this objective, we strive to make PIMD a 'Centre of distinction', an institution that promotes leadership, nurtures brilliance and turn out a pedigree of students that are of par excellence. I welcome you to our sojourn of excellence and hope it shall be a portent of many glorious chapters in your life.
